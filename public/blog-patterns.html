Drum roll please... we've partnered with Oracle Social Cloud!

Oracle Social Cloud is Oracle’s platform that helps enterprises manage and scale their customer relationships on social media channels. Our competitive analytics capabilities will now be accessible on Oracle Social Relationship Management (SRM) solution, which includes tools for social listening, publishing, engagement, analytics and workflow management.

“ We continually add capabilities to enable our customers to innovate and take advantage of real-time insights for better data-driven business decisions,” said John Stetic, Group Vice President, Oracle Marketing Cloud. “The integration of Oracle Social Cloud with Unmetric is another example of our expanding ecosystem for seamless solutions; it will provide our customers with a new way to quickly assess their marketplace and develop strategies against their competitors. ”
