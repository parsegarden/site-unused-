ABOUT UNMETRIC

We’re more that a data company.
We’re on a mission to make marketers even smarter.

What type of people are we?

The “let the girlfriend of a prospective employee interview the CEO before the boyfriend takes the job” kind. The “hangout with clients even after the party is over” kind. The “let a client sleep over at your house when he’s in town” kind. And yes, those are all true stories.

Executives

Lakshmanan Narayan

CEO & CO-FOUNDER

Lux has 18 years of experience across media, Internet, advertising and entertainment, seven of them with the Interpublic Group, and then Unmetric, which he co-founded with fellow IIT/IIM-alumni Kumar and Joe.

Kumar Krishnasami

HEAD OF PRODUCT & CO-FOUNDER

Kumar has close to 2 decades of experience, in senior technology roles and across verticals, at JP Morgan Chase, Allstate Insurance, MCI Worldcom and startups. Before Unmetric, Kumar headed Vembu Labs.

Joseph Varghese

HEAD OF TECHNOLOGY & CO-FOUNDER

Along with 18 years of experience, mostly in the SAP ecosystem as an independent consultant and with AkzoNobel, Joe also dabbled in other technology platforms and developed many products, some for his own amusement.

Jay Rampuria

HEAD OF GLOBAL CLIENT DEVELOPMENT

Over a decade in senior level account and sales management with startups as well as Nielsen and Accenture, Jay brings in considerable expertise in digital and social media analytics, having partnered with global brands and their agencies.

Tina Lakshmanan

HEAD OF FINANCE & HR

Lakyntina has over 17 years of experience in the Finance and Technology industries having worked with UTI MF, ABN AMRO and HCL. She currently has the responsibility of managing the Finance and HR functions in Unmetric Inc.
